How to use?

Way A:

* Step 1: Click "OPEN" button to choose a file to play.
* Step 2: Click "BEGIN" button or press F2 to set a beginning point.
* Step 3: Click "END" button or press F3 to set an ending point. Then Loopman will play the area between the two points while you're dictating.
* Step 4: After you finish, click "RESET" button or press ESC to cancel the two points. Then repeat Step 2 and 3 until you finish all the text.

Way B:

* Step 1: Click "OPEN" button to choose a file to play.
* Step 2: Click "REPEAT" button or press F9, Loopman will repeat the first sentence for you until you finish dictating.
* Step 3: Click "REPEAT" button or press F9 again, Loopman will go on playing.
* Step 4: Repeat Step 2 and 3 until you finish all the text.
Functions and hotkeys:

OPEN: select a file to play.
STOP/PLAY (F1): pause or play a file.
BEGIN (F2): Set beginning point.
END (F3): Set ending point.
F4: Go to beginning point.
F7: Backward 5 seconds.
F8: Forward 5 seconds.
CTRL + LEFT: Backward 1 second.
CTRL + RIGHT: Forward 1 second.
RESET (ESC): Cancel beginning and ending point.
REPEAT (F9): Repeat last part. For more information about REPEAT(F9), please read How to use?\Way B.
